1. DNO Sources

Zettar aggregates and standardizes data from all major UK Distribution Network Operators (DNOs), including network diagrams, substation locations, and available capacity. Primary sources include LTDS (Long Term Development Statements), EHV/132kV capacity maps, and known Statement of Works areas.

2. National & Regulatory Data

We incorporate spatial data from Ofgem, BEIS, and National Grid, including grid supply point locations, license area boundaries, and known constraints. Planning regulations and energy strategy papers also inform regional assumptions in our calculations.

3. Internal & Historical Records

Zettar maintains a growing database of anonymized past connection costs and project outcomes. This allows us to calibrate assumptions and compare expected reinforcement scenarios with historical precedent. Where appropriate, we blend this with live capacity and topology estimates.
