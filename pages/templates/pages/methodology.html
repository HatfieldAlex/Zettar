Our Methodology

How Zettar generates estimates and evaluates grid feasibility.

1. Data Sources

Zettar leverages public and proprietary datasets from UK Distribution Network Operators (DNOs), including Long Term Development Statements (LTDS), Statement of Works (SoW) references, and interactive capacity maps. We also ingest Ofgem regulatory documentation and national planning data to form a geospatial and technical context for each connection.
