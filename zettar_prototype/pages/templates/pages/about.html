About Zettar

Learn more about our mission, team, and what makes us different.

Who We Are

Zettar is a grid connection intelligence platform designed to streamline and simplify the process of accessing electricity networks. Built for developers, consultants, and infrastructure planners, our tool merges mapping, regulatory logic, and DNO data to deliver early, reliable insights into grid capacity and cost.

Our Mission

We aim to remove uncertainty from the grid connection process. Our mission is to empower users with data-driven clarity — helping to accelerate the deployment of EV infrastructure, battery storage, renewables, and energy-intensive developments. Zettar turns weeks of guesswork into seconds of precision.

What Makes Us Different

Zettar isn’t just a map or a static report — it’s a dynamic interface that interprets location, voltage level, and known DNO costs to give you fast, context-aware estimates. Whether you're siting a 100kW EV hub or a 50MW solar farm, Zettar adapts to your need and offers clarity before you even apply for a formal quote.
